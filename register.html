About Us

Fabric Tales EG is a family business created by five sisters in 2017. The brand started off much like many others: with social media platforms selling directly to clients who are looking for modest, stylish clothing. This presence quickly grew, with one store opening in Heliopolis and others soon to follow.


Our Product portfolio has also expanded to include perfumes, clothing fragrances, and various accessories, all under the umbrella of modestly and detailed high-quality fashion items for every occasion.
